Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dental services.

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, some patients may need more frequent visits depending on their oral health needs.

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team will be happy to verify your benefits and help you understand your coverage. We also offer flexible payment options for patients without insurance.

If you're experiencing severe pain, swelling, or have suffered trauma to your teeth, please call us immediately at (555) 123-4567. We reserve time in our schedule for emergency patients and will do our best to see you as soon as possible.

Professional teeth whitening under the supervision of a dentist is very safe. We use high-quality products and customize treatments to minimize sensitivity while achieving optimal results. Over-the-counter whitening products may not be as effective or safe.

We understand dental anxiety is common. Our team is specially trained to help anxious patients feel comfortable. We offer sedation options, explain every step of treatment, and go at your pace. Many of our formerly anxious patients now look forward to their visits!
